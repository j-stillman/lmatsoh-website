A game by Jeremiah Stillman

ABOUT

Lost Monkey and the Star of Hope is the story of Burlap, a monkey who gets lost at sea, finding himself in a land known as Gremoria. According to the first person he meets, Gremoria is in another dimension, and the only way of escaping is by touching the Star of Hope, a mystical entity.

This game is a platformer, with a story similar to that of an RPG. You, the player, control Burlap to run, jump, and climb across the land of Gremoria, while discovering its people and mysteries.
